SECTION 3: LOGO INTERPRETATION

  1. The logo of the Association shall basically be the map of the Federal Republic of Nigeria with a complete computer embedded in it.
  2. Subject to subsection (i) above, the logo shall hereinafter be known and called as the NACOSS National Logo.
  3. The logo shall be at the top-left while the School logo shall be at the top-right and the NACOSS Chapter logo shall be the background of the letter headed paper and other documents of the Association.
